Our Story

Your new favorite neighborhood coffee spot

Founded in 2025, Morgenmuffel began with a simple mission: to create a space where exceptional coffee and genuine connection could flourish. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms.

Our baristas are craftspeople, treating each brew as an opportunity to perfect their art — from the first pour to the final presentation.

Beyond coffee, our kitchen produces fresh, seasonal dishes using locally-sourced ingredients.
